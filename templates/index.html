Academic Excellence.

librika.in was designed to bridge the gap between physical inventory and digital tracking. Providing real-time insights, automated barcode systems, and professional management tools.

Core Features & Functions:

  • ✅ Automated Cataloging: Instant AI-powered barcode scanning and book data extraction.
  • ✅ Digital Circulation: Seamless check-in and check-out tracking for students and staff.
  • ✅ Smart Analytics: Real-time dashboards monitoring overdue books and reading trends.
  • ✅ E-Library Integration: Direct access to digital PDFs and media content alongside physical books.
  • ✅ Role-Based Access: Dedicated secure portals for Super Admins, Librarians, and Students.
